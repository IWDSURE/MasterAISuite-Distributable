# HWM_TM_REP_WORK_TM_TCAT_V

## Details

**Schema:** FUSION

**Object owner:** HWM

**Object type:** VIEW

**Source:** [https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/human-resources/oedmh/hwmtmrepworktmtcatv-3815.html#hwmtmrepworktmtcatv-3815](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/human-resources/oedmh/hwmtmrepworktmtcatv-3815.html#hwmtmrepworktmtcatv-3815)

## Columns

- PERSON_ID
- ASSIGNMENT_ID
- MEASURE
- UNIT_OF_MEASURE
- START_TIME
- STOP_TIME
- TM_REC_ID
- TM_REC_VERSION
- DAY_GRP_TYPE_ID
- TCAT_ID
- STATUS_DATE_FROM
- STATUS_DATE_TO
- STATUS_VALUE
- STATUS_DEF_CD

## Query

```sql
select TIME_RECORDS.PERSON_ID , TIME_RECORDS.ASSIGNMENT_ID , TIME_RECORDS.MEASURE , TIME_RECORDS.UNIT_OF_MEASURE , TIME_RECORDS.START_TIME , TIME_RECORDS.STOP_TIME , TIME_RECORDS.TM_REC_ID , TIME_RECORDS.TM_REC_VERSION , TIME_RECORDS.DAY_GRP_TYPE_ID , TCAT_USG.TCAT_ID , TIME_RECORDS.STATUS_DATE_FROM , TIME_RECORDS.STATUS_DATE_TO , TIME_RECORDS.STATUS_VALUE , STATUS_DEF.STATUS_DEF_CD from HWM_TCAT_USAGES TCAT_USG, HWM_TM_REP_WORK_TM_CORE_V TIME_RECORDS, HWM_TM_STATUS_DEF_B STATUS_DEF where TCAT_USG.TM_BLDG_BLK_ID = TIME_RECORDS.TM_REC_ID AND TCAT_USG.TM_BLDG_BLK_VERSION = TIME_RECORDS.TM_REC_VERSION AND STATUS_DEF.TM_STATUS_DEF_ID = TIME_RECORDS.TM_STATUS_DEF_ID
```
