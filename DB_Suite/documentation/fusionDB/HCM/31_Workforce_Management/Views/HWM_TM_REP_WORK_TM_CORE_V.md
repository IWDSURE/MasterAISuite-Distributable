# HWM_TM_REP_WORK_TM_CORE_V

## Details

**Schema:** FUSION

**Object owner:** HWM

**Object type:** VIEW

**Source:** [https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/human-resources/oedmh/hwmtmrepworktmcorev-4102.html#hwmtmrepworktmcorev-4102](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/human-resources/oedmh/hwmtmrepworktmcorev-4102.html#hwmtmrepworktmcorev-4102)

## Columns

- PERSON_ID
- ASSIGNMENT_ID
- MEASURE
- UNIT_OF_MEASURE
- START_TIME
- STOP_TIME
- TM_REC_ID
- TM_REC_VERSION
- DAY_GRP_TYPE_ID
- STATUS_DATE_FROM
- STATUS_DATE_TO
- STATUS_VALUE
- TM_STATUS_DEF_ID
- ORIG_TM_REC_ID
- ORIG_TM_REC_VERSION

## Query

```sql
SELECT TIME_RECORDS.RESOURCE_ID as PERSON_ID , TIME_RECORDS.SUBRESOURCE_ID as ASSIGNMENT_ID , TIME_RECORDS.MEASURE , TIME_RECORDS.UNIT_OF_MEASURE , TIME_RECORDS.START_TIME , TIME_RECORDS.STOP_TIME , TIME_RECORDS.TM_REC_ID , TIME_RECORDS.TM_REC_VERSION , DAY_REC.GRP_TYPE_ID DAY_GRP_TYPE_ID , STATUS_VAL.DATE_FROM as STATUS_DATE_FROM , STATUS_VAL.DATE_TO as STATUS_DATE_TO , STATUS_VAL.STATUS_VALUE ,STATUS_VAL.TM_STATUS_DEF_ID , TIME_RECORDS.ORIG_TM_REC_ID as ORIG_TM_REC_ID , TIME_RECORDS.ORIG_TM_REC_VERSION as ORIG_TM_REC_VERSION from HWM_TM_REC TIME_RECORDS, HWM_TM_REC_GRP_USAGES GROUP_USAGES, HWM_TM_REC_GRP TIME_CARD, HWM_TM_STATUSES STATUS_VAL , HWM_TM_REC_GRP DAY_REC where GROUP_USAGES.TM_REC_ID = TIME_RECORDS.TM_REC_ID AND GROUP_USAGES.TM_REC_VERSION = TIME_RECORDS.TM_REC_VERSION AND GROUP_USAGES.LATEST_VERSION = 'Y' AND DAY_REC.TM_REC_GRP_ID = GROUP_USAGES.TM_REC_GRP_ID AND DAY_REC.TM_REC_GRP_VERSION = GROUP_USAGES.TM_REC_GRP_VERSION AND (NVL(DAY_REC.DELETE_FLAG,'N')) = 'N' AND DAY_REC.LATEST_VERSION = 'Y' AND TIME_CARD.TM_REC_GRP_ID = DAY_REC.PARENT_TM_REC_GRP_ID AND TIME_CARD.TM_REC_GRP_VERSION = DAY_REC.PARENT_TM_REC_GRP_VERSION AND (NVL(TIME_CARD.DELETE_FLAG,'N')) = 'N' AND TIME_CARD.LATEST_VERSION = 'Y' AND ( ( DAY_REC.GRP_TYPE_ID = '101' AND TIME_CARD.TM_REC_GRP_ID = STATUS_VAL.TM_BLDG_BLK_ID AND TIME_CARD.TM_REC_GRP_VERSION = STATUS_VAL.TM_BLDG_BLK_VERSION ) OR ( DAY_REC.GRP_TYPE_ID = '201' AND TIME_CARD.ORIG_TM_REC_GRP_ID = STATUS_VAL.TM_BLDG_BLK_ID AND TIME_CARD.ORIG_TM_REC_GRP_VERSION = STATUS_VAL.TM_BLDG_BLK_VERSION ) ) AND TIME_RECORDS.RESOURCE_TYPE = 'PERSON'
```
