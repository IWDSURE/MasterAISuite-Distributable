# HWR_RECOGNITION_VL

## Details

**Schema:** FUSION

**Object owner:** HWR

**Object type:** VIEW

**Source:** [https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/human-resources/oedmh/hwrrecognitionvl-3397.html#hwrrecognitionvl-3397](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/human-resources/oedmh/hwrrecognitionvl-3397.html#hwrrecognitionvl-3397)

## Columns

- RECOGNITION_ID
- RECOGNITION_TEXT
- RECOGNITION_DATE
- RECOMMENDER_ID
- RECOMMENDER_GBL_PRFL_ID
- RECOMMENDER_NAME
- RECOMMENDER_TITLE
- RECOMMENDER_EMAIL
- RECOMMENDER_RELATION
- RECOMMENDER_IMAGE_URL
- GBL_PRFL_ID
- CREATED_BY
- CREATION_DATE
- LAST_UPDATED_BY
- LAST_UPDATE_DATE
- LAST_UPDATE_LOGIN

## Query

```sql
SELECT B.RECOGNITION_ID , B.RECOGNITION_TEXT , B.RECOGNITION_DATE , B.RECOMMENDER_ID , B.RECOMMENDER_GBL_PRFL_ID , B.RECOMMENDER_NAME , B.RECOMMENDER_TITLE , B.RECOMMENDER_EMAIL , B.RECOMMENDER_RELATION , B.RECOMMENDER_IMAGE_URL , X.GBL_PRFL_ID , B.CREATED_BY , B.CREATION_DATE , B.LAST_UPDATED_BY , B.LAST_UPDATE_DATE , B.LAST_UPDATE_LOGIN FROM HWR_RECOGNITION_B B LEFT JOIN HWR_RECOGN_GBL_PRFL_X X ON (B.RECOGNITION_ID = X.RECOGNITION_ID)
```
