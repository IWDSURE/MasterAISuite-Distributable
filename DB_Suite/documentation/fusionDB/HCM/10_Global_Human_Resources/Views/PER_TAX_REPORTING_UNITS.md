# PER_TAX_REPORTING_UNITS

## Details

**Schema:** FUSION

**Object owner:** PER

**Object type:** VIEW

**Source:** [https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/human-resources/oedmh/pertaxreportingunits-7428.html#pertaxreportingunits-7428](https://docs.oracle.com/en/cloud/saas/human-resources/oedmh/pertaxreportingunits-7428.html#pertaxreportingunits-7428)

## Columns

- ESTABLISHMENT_ID
- ORGANIZATION_ID
- EFFECTIVE_FROM
- EFFECTIVE_TO
- ESTAB_NAME
- MAIN_ESTABLISHMENT_FLAG
- PARENT_PSU_ORG_ID
- PARENT_PSU_NAME
- ENTERPRISE_ID

## Query

```sql
SELECT ESTABLISHMENT.ESTABLISHMENT_ID, ORG_UNIT_ESTAB.ORGANIZATION_ID, ESTABLISHMENT.EFFECTIVE_FROM, ESTABLISHMENT.EFFECTIVE_TO, ESTABLISHMENT.NAME ESTAB_NAME, ESTABLISHMENT.MAIN_ESTABLISHMENT_FLAG, ORG_UNIT_PSU.ORGANIZATION_ID PARENT_PSU_ORG_ID, XLE_LE.NAME PARENT_PSU_NAME, XLE_LE.ENTERPRISE_ID ENTERPRISE_ID FROM XLE_ETB_PROFILES ESTABLISHMENT, HR_ALL_ORGANIZATION_UNITS_F_VL ORG_UNIT_ESTAB, HR_ORG_UNIT_CLASSIFICATIONS_F ORG_CLASS_ESTAB, XLE_ENTITY_PROFILES XLE_LE, HR_ALL_ORGANIZATION_UNITS_F_VL ORG_UNIT_PSU, HR_ORG_UNIT_CLASSIFICATIONS_F ORG_CLASS_PSU WHERE ESTABLISHMENT.ESTABLISHMENT_ID = ORG_UNIT_ESTAB.ESTABLISHMENT_ID AND ORG_UNIT_ESTAB.ORGANIZATION_ID = ORG_CLASS_ESTAB.ORGANIZATION_ID AND TRUNC(SYSDATE) BETWEEN ORG_CLASS_ESTAB.EFFECTIVE_START_DATE AND ORG_CLASS_ESTAB.EFFECTIVE_END_DATE AND TRUNC(SYSDATE) BETWEEN ORG_UNIT_ESTAB.EFFECTIVE_START_DATE AND ORG_UNIT_ESTAB.EFFECTIVE_END_DATE AND ORG_CLASS_ESTAB.CLASSIFICATION_CODE = 'HCM_TRU' AND XLE_LE.LEGAL_ENTITY_ID = ESTABLISHMENT.LEGAL_ENTITY_ID AND ORG_UNIT_PSU.LEGAL_ENTITY_ID = NVL(XLE_LE.PARENT_PSU_ID,XLE_LE.LEGAL_ENTITY_ID) AND ORG_UNIT_PSU.ORGANIZATION_ID = ORG_CLASS_PSU.ORGANIZATION_ID AND TRUNC(SYSDATE) BETWEEN ORG_CLASS_PSU.EFFECTIVE_START_DATE AND ORG_CLASS_PSU.EFFECTIVE_END_DATE AND TRUNC(SYSDATE) BETWEEN ORG_UNIT_PSU.EFFECTIVE_START_DATE AND ORG_UNIT_PSU.EFFECTIVE_END_DATE AND ORG_CLASS_PSU.CLASSIFICATION_CODE = 'HCM_PSU' AND XLE_LE.PSU_FLAG = 'Y'
```
